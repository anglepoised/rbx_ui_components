The rb-main component should be used to wrap the main content of the page i.e. any components not contained in rb-header or rb-footer.

Two stackable modifiers are available:

  • .Main--background: sets a covering background image.
  • .Main--center: vertically and horizontally centers all children.

When modifiers are used:

  • rb-main must be a direct child of the rg-site component.
  • .Main--background should not be used without also using .Main--center.
