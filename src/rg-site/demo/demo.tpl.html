The rg-site component should wrap all content on a page and is used in conjunction with the rg-site-top (top, sticky), rg-site-middle (middle, scrollable) and rg-site-bottom (bottom, sticky) descendents to create the desired layout. rg-site-top would ideally be used in conjunction with rb-main to achieve centering content effects.

Using all three subcomponents, the rg-site-top and rg-site-bottom sections will be sticky, and the scrollable rg-site-middle will fill the remainder of the space.
